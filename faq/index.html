FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is DFC?

We are a family owned tax and financial advisement company based in the Coachella Valley.

What kind of services do you provide?

We primarily provide tax services, such as tax preperations and deductions. However our team is also specialized and experienced in retirement planning, insurance, immigration, and estate management.

How long has DFC been in operation?

We were founded in 1996, right here in the Coachella Valley. We have been providing our services for over years.

Do you take appointments?

All appointments are made via walk-ins or by giving us a call. It is highly advisible to visit us in either of our offices.

Where are you located?

We currently have offices located in Indio and Coachella. Please visit Locations to get more information about our office locations.

Have you always been family ran?

Yes. Since 1996 we have been a family operated business.
